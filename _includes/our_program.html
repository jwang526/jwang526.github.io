Our Mission

Music For is a nonprofit organization based in Northern Indiana aiming to provide student musicians with the opportunity to engage and enrich the community through live music performance.



Music For... Who?

Well... everyone. As an organization, our goal is to provide the amazing gift of music to everyone in the community, for free- but we especially aim to target those who may benefit the most from music, including those who may not usually have the opportunity to enjoy music because of age or medical reasons, as well as children who get to be introduced to the amazing world that music can be. We work with local student musicians, encouraging them to use their incredible musical talent for a good cause- and we are ever grateful for their willingness and dedication.
