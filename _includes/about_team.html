Suzana Josenhans

Second Violinist


Suzana Josenhans began playing violin as a three-year-old with the Suzuki program in Evansville, Indiana. Under the patient guidance of her private teacher, group lessons, and practicing, Suzana learned the fundamentals of violin technique and the art of listening. At the age of 10, Suzana began playing in the Evansville Philharmonic Youth Orchestra, where she served as concertmaster of the younger ensemble for the 2019-2020 season. She went on to play first violin in the large ensemble. Upon moving to South Bend, Indiana in July 2022, Suzana became a member of the Symphonic Orchestra at Penn High School and of the South Bend Youth Symphony Orchestra, serving in the first violin sections of both orchestras. She currently studies with Aviva Hakanoglu.
